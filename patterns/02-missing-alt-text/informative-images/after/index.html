ProSound X1 Headphones: Full Review

By Alex Morgan · April 16, 2026 · Hardware

The ProSound X1 is an over-ear wireless headphone with 40-hour battery life, active noise cancellation, and memory-foam ear cushions. At $149 it sits in the sweet spot between budget and premium.

Build quality is excellent. The midnight-black finish resists smudges and the stainless headband adjusts smoothly across head sizes.

Battery Life Comparison

The 40-hour battery is class-leading at this price point. Competitors typically offer 22–30 hours. Charging takes 90 minutes via USB-C.

How to Pair via Bluetooth

Pairing is fast and reliable. The headphones remembered up to eight previously connected devices and switched between them seamlessly.
