Product Description

This premium wireless keyboard features mechanical switches, RGB backlighting, and a compact design perfect for both work and gaming. The durable aluminum frame ensures longevity, while the hot-swappable switches allow for easy customization.

Includes a detachable USB-C cable, keycap puller, and switch removal tool. Compatible with Windows, macOS, and Linux.
